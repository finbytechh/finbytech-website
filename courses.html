Premium 3-Module Program Structure covering Stocks, Equity, Futures, Options & MCX

  • 📘 Module 1: Market Foundations (Beginner Level)
  • 📗 Module 2: Trading & Investing Strategies (Intermediate Level)
  • 📙 Module 3: Professional Mastery (Advanced Level)

Module 1: Market Foundations (Beginner Level)

✅ Goal: Build strong basics & understand how markets work.
✅ Introduction to financial markets (Stocks, Equity, MCX, F&O)
✅ Order types & trading platforms (Market, Limit, SL, Bracket, Cover)
✅ Equity basics: Delivery vs. Intraday trading
✅ Corporate actions (Dividends, Splits, Rights, Bonus)
✅ Candlestick basics, Support & Resistance, Trendlines
✅ Simple indicators (Moving Averages, RSI, MACD)
✅ Basics of Fundamental Analysis (Financial statements, simple ratios)
✅ Risk management fundamentals (Stop-loss, position sizing, capital safety)

 Module 2: Trading & Investing Strategies (Intermediate Level)

✅ Goal: Learn practical tools & strategies for real-world trading.
✅ Advanced charting (patterns, breakouts, volume analysis)
✅ Futures trading (Lot size, margin, rollover, open interest)
✅ Options basics (Calls, Puts, Option chain, ITM/ATM/OTM)
✅ Beginner options strategies (Covered Call, Bull Call Spread, Protective Put)
✅ MCX basics (Gold, Silver, Crude Oil, Agri commodities)
✅ Equity investing strategies (Growth vs. Value, Sector analysis, Portfolio diversification)
✅ Swing & positional trading setups
✅ Risk-reward ratios, trade journaling & consistency building

 Module 3: Professional Mastery (Advanced Level)

✅ Goal: Trade like institutions & manage multi-asset portfolios.
✅ Market Microstructure & Order Flow insights
✅ Option Greeks (Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega) with real use cases
✅ Implied Volatility (IV), Skew & Volatility trading
✅ Advanced Option Strategies (Straddle, Strangle, Iron Condor, Butterfly, Ratio, Calendar spreads)
✅ Futures arbitrage & spread trading (Index, Stock, Commodities)
✅ Advanced Commodity trading (Contango, Backwardation, Global correlations)
✅ Quantitative & Algo basics (Backtesting, Rule-based strategies)
✅ Portfolio Management (MPT, CAPM, Hedging with derivatives)
✅ Pro-level risk management (Kelly Criterion, Drawdown control)
✅ Trading psychology & behavioral finance
✅ Back market practice (Intraday scalping, swing trades, event-based strategies)
