What is Intense Dating?

Intense Dating is a premium dating application that helps you find meaningful connections. It features Private Chat, Video Calling, Voice Calling, Verified Profiles, AI Matchmaking, and Premium Membership options to enhance your dating experience.

Is Intense Dating free to use?

Intense Dating offers both free and premium features. Basic features like creating a profile and browsing matches are free. Premium features such as unlimited likes, advanced filters, and priority support require a Premium Membership subscription.

How does the verification process work?

Our verification process ensures authentic profiles. Users can verify their identity through phone number verification, photo verification, and social media linking. Verified profiles receive a badge indicating their authenticity.
